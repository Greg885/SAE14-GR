Having started with soccer like most young boys, I found my path in handball when I was 10 years old. Encouraged by my parents to resume sports after a long-term injury, I began practicing this sport at the Handball Club de Village-Neuf (HCVN), where I took my first steps in a team at the departmental level. The following year, despite a move to a higher division, the atmosphere didn't seem optimal to me. So, I made the decision to join another club. It was TV Klein-Basel in Switzerland that welcomed me and allowed me to acquire new skills, both in sports and linguistics. Playing in a Pre-national championship, we traveled across the country, covering thousands of kilometers by train. The atmosphere, coaches, and team spirit allowed me to flourish fully for 2 seasons. As the youngest player on the team, I am slowly finding my footing in more intense and rough matches, always supported and encouraged by teammates and numerous supporters making their voices heard in the stands.

Handball... my life, my passion!
